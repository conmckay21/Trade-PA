# Changelog

## 2026-05-31

### 📞 New: Calls
- Every call in and out, all in one place under People → Calls.
- Don't recognise a number? It's flagged, so new enquiries are easy to spot.
- Tap any call to see what it was about, read the transcript, and play the recording back.
- Worth chasing? One tap logs them as an enquiry or saves them as a customer, and any past calls from that number get tucked under their name.
