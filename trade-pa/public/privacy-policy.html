Overview

Trade PA is an administration tool for sole-trader tradespeople in the United Kingdom. It helps tradespeople manage jobs, invoices, quotes, materials, customers, reminders, and email communications from a single app.

We are committed to protecting your personal data and handling it responsibly. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Trade PA is designed so that your business data belongs to you. We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than operating the service you have signed up for.

Data we collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the app. This falls into three categories:

Account data
  • Email address and password (used for login via Supabase Auth)
  • Business name and trading name
  • Business address, phone number, and email
  • Bank details you enter for display on invoices (stored securely, never used for transactions)
  • VAT number and Gas Safe number if provided
Business data
  • Jobs, schedules, and appointment details
  • Invoices and quotes including customer names and addresses
  • Materials lists and supplier information
  • Customer contact records
  • Reminders and notes
Technical data
  • IP address and browser type (collected automatically by our hosting provider)
  • App usage activity for the purpose of diagnosing errors
  • OAuth tokens if you connect a Gmail or Outlook account (see section 03)

Gmail & email access

Trade PA offers an optional feature to connect your Gmail or Microsoft Outlook account. This connection is entirely optional — the core app functions fully without it.

If you choose to connect your email account, Trade PA will request the following permissions:

Gmail permissions requested
  • gmail.readonly — to read your inbox so you can view emails inside Trade PA
  • gmail.send — to send invoices, quotes, and payment chasers directly from the app
  • gmail.modify — to mark emails as read when you open them inside Trade PA
Trade PA's use of data obtained from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not use your Gmail data to develop, improve, or train generalised AI or machine learning models.

Your Gmail OAuth tokens are stored securely in our database (Supabase, hosted in the EU) and are used only to fetch and send emails on your explicit instruction within the app. We do not read, store, or process the content of your emails for any purpose other than displaying them to you.

You can disconnect your Gmail or Outlook account at any time from within the app settings. When disconnected, your OAuth tokens are deleted from our systems immediately.

How we use your data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Trade PA service:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure
  • To store and display your jobs, invoices, customers, and other business records
  • To generate invoice and quote PDFs
  • To send emails on your instruction via your connected email account
  • To sync invoices with connected accounting software (Xero, QuickBooks) if you have enabled this
  • To process voice notes via OpenAI Whisper transcription (audio is sent to OpenAI and immediately discarded after transcription)
  • To power AI assistant features via the Anthropic Claude API (your prompts and context are sent to Anthropic for processing and are subject to Anthropic's privacy policy)

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the third-party services necessary to run Trade PA:

Third-party processors
  • Supabase — database and authentication (EU-hosted). Privacy policy →
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions. Privacy policy →
  • Anthropic — AI assistant (Claude API). Privacy policy →
  • OpenAI — voice transcription (Whisper API). Privacy policy →
  • Google — Gmail OAuth (if you connect your Gmail). Privacy policy →
  • Microsoft — Outlook OAuth (if you connect your Outlook). Privacy policy →
  • Xero / QuickBooks — accounting sync (if you connect these services)

We do not share your data with any other third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

Data storage & security

Your data is stored in Supabase, which is hosted within the European Union. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. All data at rest is encrypted by Supabase.

Access to your data is protected by Supabase Row Level Security — this means your data is only accessible when authenticated as you. No other Trade PA user can access your business records.

We use industry-standard security practices but no system is completely immune to risk. We recommend using a strong, unique password for your Trade PA account.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your personal data and business records are permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

Backups may retain data for up to 90 days after deletion, after which they are permanently purged.

OAuth tokens for connected email accounts are deleted immediately when you disconnect the account.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can correct inaccurate data directly in the app or by contacting us
  • Right to erasure — you can request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Right to portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — you can object to how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in section 11.

Cookies

Trade PA uses only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

The authentication session cookie is set by Supabase and is necessary for you to remain logged in to the app.

Changes to this policy

Continued use of Trade PA after changes to this policy constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
