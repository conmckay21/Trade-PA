About Trade PA

Built by trade, for trade.

Trade PA wasn't dreamt up in a tech office. It started with a builder's van, a whiteboard diary, and too many Sunday evenings spent typing up the week.

Trade PA was founded by someone who ran a small building firm with subcontractors. The work was good. The admin was the problem: quotes typed up at the kitchen table, invoices chased at 9pm, CIS statements wrestled into spreadsheets, RAMS written from scratch for every job that needed one.

The apps on the market didn't fix it. They were forms on a screen, built for office managers, priced per user, with the UK-specific stuff bolted on through integrations. None of them did the work. They just gave the work a login.

So we built an assistant instead of another form. Her name is Eve.

Eve does what a really good office manager would do: takes what you say in plain English and turns it into the quote, the invoice, the booking, the chase, the RAMS, the mileage log. You stay on the tools, she does the paperwork, and you check her work in one tap.

Trade PA is built in Portsmouth, hosted securely, and developed with UK trade compliance at the core: CIS, Domestic Reverse Charge, HMRC mileage, RAMS and trade certificates are native, not afterthoughts.
