What is Trade PA?

Trade PA is a web-based business administration application designed specifically for sole-trader tradespeople operating in the United Kingdom. It provides a single platform to manage the administrative side of a trades business, replacing spreadsheets, paper invoices, and separate apps.

The application includes tools for job scheduling, invoice and quote generation, customer contact management, materials and stock tracking, payment reminders, and an AI-powered voice assistant that lets tradespeople log jobs and create invoices hands-free while on site.

Trade PA optionally integrates with Gmail and Microsoft Outlook to allow tradespeople to send invoices directly from the app, chase outstanding payments, and automatically import supplier invoices into the materials tracker. It also integrates with Xero and QuickBooks for accounting synchronisation.
