Common questions

Straight answers.

How does the 30-day free trial work?

Sign up with your email, no card needed. You get 30 days with everything unlocked and a taster allowance of 50 conversations and 30 minutes hands-free. To keep going after day 30, add a card and pick your plan. Don't, and your trial simply ends. You'll never be charged without adding a card yourself.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are monthly rolling. Cancel and you keep access until the end of the billing period, with no further charges. Cancellation is one tap in the app under Settings, Plan & billing.

What happens if I hit my monthly AI limit?

Three options: upgrade, buy a one-off top-up, or wait for your monthly reset. The app shows your usage so you'll see it coming. Tap-to-talk voice fill on forms is never capped.

Any hidden fees?

No. Your plan fee includes everything: all 43 features, your AI allowance and your hands-free time. The only extras are optional: usage top-ups and the Business Phone add-on.

Do you integrate with Xero or QuickBooks?

Yes, both. Invoices and supplier bills sync automatically once connected. No double entry, clean books for your accountant.

Can I switch plans later?

Any time, either direction, prorated automatically. No penalties, no lock-ins.

I'm VAT registered. Can I reclaim VAT on the subscription?

Yes. VAT invoices are downloadable from the billing portal, ready for your accountant.

iPhone or Android?

Both, natively, from the App Store and Google Play, plus a web app for desktop. Same features everywhere.

Is my data private?

Yes. UK GDPR compliant, encrypted at rest and in transit, never sold or shared, deletable on request. Details in our privacy policy.
